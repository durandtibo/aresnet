# Proposed Library Names for aresnet

This document presents a curated list of proposed names to potentially replace `aresnet`. All names meet the following criteria:
- Maximum 10 characters in length
- No underscores (`_`) or hyphens (`-`)
- Available on PyPI (verified on 2026-01-30)

## Top Recommendations

### 1. **aresilient** ⭐
- **Meaning**: Combines "ares" (Greek god of courage) with "resilient"
- **Rationale**: Perfectly captures the library's core purpose (resilient HTTP requests) while maintaining the "ares" prefix
- **Memorability**: High - the word "resilient" is directly related to the library's functionality
- **Availability**: ✅ Available on PyPI

### 2. **aresretry** ⭐
- **Meaning**: Combines "ares" with "retry"
- **Rationale**: Clear and descriptive - immediately conveys the retry mechanism
- **Memorability**: High - straightforward and descriptive
- **Availability**: ✅ Available on PyPI

### 3. **httpguard**
- **Meaning**: HTTP guardian/protector
- **Rationale**: Suggests protection and reliability for HTTP requests
- **Memorability**: High - evokes safety and dependability
- **Availability**: ✅ Available on PyPI

### 4. **resilhttp**
- **Meaning**: Resilient HTTP
- **Rationale**: Compact combination that clearly describes the library's purpose
- **Memorability**: Medium-High - straightforward but slightly harder to pronounce
- **Availability**: ✅ Available on PyPI

### 5. **httpback**
- **Meaning**: HTTP with backoff (exponential backoff)
- **Rationale**: References the key exponential backoff feature
- **Memorability**: Medium-High - "back" could refer to backoff
- **Availability**: ✅ Available on PyPI
